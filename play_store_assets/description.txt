## Full Description (4000 characters max)
EventSnap is the ultimate photo-sharing solution for all your events - from casual gatherings to life's biggest celebrations. Never miss a moment again!

### Key Features:

✅ **Easy Event Creation**: Set up an event in seconds with a custom name and join code.

✅ **Secure Photo Sharing**: Only invited participants can view and upload photos to your events.

✅ **Real-time Photo Gallery**: See photos appear instantly as they're uploaded by event participants.

✅ **Direct Camera Access**: Take photos within the app and share them with one tap.

✅ **Photo Library Integration**: Choose existing photos from your device to share with the group.

✅ **User-friendly Interface**: Simple, intuitive design that anyone can use.

✅ **Participant Management**: See who's joined your event and manage the guest list.

✅ **Free to Use**: All core features are available without any subscription fees.
